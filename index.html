Key Financial Formula

Return on Equity (ROE):

\[ \mathrm{ROE} = \dfrac{\mathrm{Net\ Income}}{\mathrm{Shareholders'\ Equity}} \times 100\% \]

Example: if Net Income = $10m and Equity = $80m, ROE = 12.5%.
